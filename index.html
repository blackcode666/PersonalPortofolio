About

A hybrid approach to design and code.

I help small businesses and startups turn ideas into functional, good-looking digital products — from responsive websites to visual design assets.

What I do:

  • Frontend development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • UI/UX and graphic design using Figma, Canva, Framer, Inkscape, and Photopea
  • AI-assisted workflows to move faster without compromising quality — with full transparency to clients
  • Organized project scoping and timelines using Microsoft Excel

I'm currently working with early clients on website and design projects, alongside an ongoing portfolio of self-directed builds where I practice solving real business problems — clean layouts, mobile responsiveness, and visuals that support a brand's goals.
